Listen. Learn. Laugh.

Life the Universe and Everything is a board game in development.

It is a tool for use by churches, bible study groups, youth groups, and others to bring people together and start meaningful conversations.

Do converstations often feel shallow?

Do you want to talk about God, the bible, religion, ethics, and the big things that really matter?

To you want to have your friends, neighbours, family over for dinner or a BBQ and have a real discussion about eternity, heaven and hell?

Life the Universe and Everything is being carefully researched and designed to foster fun and conversation.

Created by Experts

Developed by Tim Roediger, and Marty Olmos. Tim is an award winning game designer, with a Bachelor of Theology. Marty is an acknowledged expert in Educational Technology and administers online theological training for Moore Theological College, Sydney.

Easy to play

A game of Life and Universe and Everything will bring a spark of fun, listening, learning and insight to your youth group, bible study, or church gathering. It will foster honest sharing about beliefs, and provide a foundation for deeper discussion.

Life the Universe and Everything has already been played many times, and continues to be developed into a polished product.

Different from anything Else

Inspired by conversation games like the Ungame, Compatability, Dixit and The Art of Conversation, Life the Universe and Everything stands out in two ways:

  • Unlike products like the Ungame and The Art of Conversation, Life the Universe and Everything is a real game with an optional scoring system. Play can include the fun and excitement of competition, winning, and losing. Scoring is designed so it is not based on luck, but also does not hinder quality discussion.
  • Unlike the other products, Life the Universe and Everything allows you to set the discussion agenda. Whilst the game comes with a wide range of suggested discussion topics, it is trivial, and encouraged to introduce your own.
