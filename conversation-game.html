Listen. Learn. Laugh.

Life the Universe and Everything is a board game in development.

It is being carefully researched and designed to foster fun and conversation.

Developed by award winning game designer Tim Roediger, and acknowledged expert in Educational Technology Marty Olmos, Life the Universe and Everything is a game that combines fun, laughs, listening, and the exploration of serious topics. It creates the environment for quality conversations with a smile.

The perfect way to break the ice with a group, make family dinners and barbeques memorable, or broach difficult topics in an easy, friendly, approachable manner.

A game of Life and Universe and Everything will bring a spark of fun, listening, learning and insight to any group, both young and old.

No knowledge is required to play, it is all about listening to and sharing your own interests, beliefs, and life experiences.

Life the Universe and Everything has already been played many times, and continues to be developed into a commercial product.

Inspired by forerunners like the Ungame, Compatability, Dixit and The Art of Conversation, Life the Universe and Everything stands out in two ways:

  • Unlike products like the Ungame and The Art of Conversation, Life the Universe and Everything is a real game with an optional scoring system. Play can include the fun and excitement of competition, winning, and losing. Scoring is designed so it is not based on luck, but also does not hinder quality discussion.
  • Unlike all the other products listed, Life the Universe and Everything allows you to set the discussion agenda. Whilst the game comes with a wide range of suggested discussion topics, it is trivial, and encouraged to introduce your own.
